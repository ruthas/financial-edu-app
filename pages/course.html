This section explores your personal history with money. Uncover why you do what you do that causes you to get the results you’re getting with money so that you can change what needs to be changed and strengthen what you’re doing correctly to improve your money results.
You’ll learn why your underlying feelings and subconscious thoughts govern your financial life and just when and where it all started. We dig up your money emotional roots to expose how social norms, programming and the old paradigm affects us in our daily lives and how to reawaken from the deep sleep we’ve been lulled into surrounding money.
Your understanding of money will soar beyond getting it and spending it! You’ll become aligned with money on an intellectual and energetic level.
You’ll also learn the true value a dollar. What money is and what it isn’t where true economic value is hiding.
In this section I introduce a topic called Money Meta-communication. We’ll take a look at how you learned money without words but through energetic and physical teachings that have stuck with you from childhood. I’ll share a portion of my own money story and I’ll talk about how emotions like guilt and shame keep money out of reach for many people.
I was fortunate enough to learn some very valuable money lessons from a Billionaire and I’m going to share those lessons with you.
